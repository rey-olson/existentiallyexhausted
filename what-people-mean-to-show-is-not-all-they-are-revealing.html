"There is truth, and there are lies. People are still interested in knowing the difference."

A phenomenal book about Eliot Higgins and the creation of Bellingcat. A must read for everyone interested in the OSINT space.

I found it uplifting to read his rather optimistic convictions in this supposedly "post-truth" world, enflamed by misinformation and disinformation.

The books contains lots and lots of interesting tidbits. My notes from the book (in no particular order):

  • The Interpreter (anti-Kremlin)
  • Trolls in your feed (Trolde i dit feed)
  • First Draft News, Snopes
  • Faktenfinder, Les Décodeurs, Reporter's Lab (Duke University)
  • Bellingchat Podcast
  • Armed Conflict Location & Event Data
  • Syrian Archive, Yemeni Archive, Mnemonic
  • Amnesty International - Decoders platform
