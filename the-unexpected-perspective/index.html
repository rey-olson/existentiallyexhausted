The Unexpected Perspective: Conversations About Church and Meaning.

After my grandfather -a very kind and religious man- died, this was one of his books I salvaged from his bookshelf. 

"Post-corona" life is still kicking me down (still more than I care to admit), so I turned to this book to get my mind onto something else. 

I actually found it superbly refreshing to read about meaningful *badum tss* conversations about faith. Where I normally limit my main reading to security, cyber, tech, war or espionage.

Conclusion: I plan to switch more often between book subjects or even genres. Every other book needs to be something non-tech related.

Hell, even Lady Gaga sings about faith! (which reminds me: I should start making posts about artists or songs that I like)
