The Invisible War

I received the book as a gift from Fox-IT. They use it as marketing material, since the company plays a prominent role in a variety of high-profile cases discussed in the book.

It is a well-written analysis on a selection of recent breaches or hacks. I am also very happy to see a publication on these topics in Dutch.

Be aware that the book heavily focuses on the issues or challenges intelligence agencies have with our new 'cyber' reality.
