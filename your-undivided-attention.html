Your Undivided Attention 

Your Undivided Attention is a brilliant, and honestly mind-blowing, podcast series about the issues surrounding the marriage between modern technology and the attention economy. The format is perfect and the invited speakers are top-notch.

The Netflix documentary 'The Social Dilemma' is a watered-down version of the ideas behind the podcast, but it makes the topic accessible for a wider audience.
