Dune

I really liked the film (who doesn't love Dennis Villeneuve master pieces?!) and as I hadn't read the book, I went and bought the first print version I could get my hands on. This happened to be a Dutch 'film edition' translation. It is a combination of the first two Dune novels 'Dune World' and 'The Prophet of Dune'.

Thoughts

  • I am quite amazed how closely the movie adaptation follows the storylines and details of the book. Impressive to say the least.
  • It's a long and slow read. Not that much 'action' happens over so many pages, but it's a captivating and entertaining read nevertheless.
