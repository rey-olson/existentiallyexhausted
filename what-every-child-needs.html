'Wat elk kind nodig heeft' or 'What every child needs' by Klaar Hammenecker. She is a child psychologist, psychotherapist¬† and family mediator.

Anyway, here's a (Google Translated) summary:

What every child needs 5 Basics for Powerful and Developmental Parenting

The answer to parenting stress: powerful parenting

The 5 BASICS for Powerful Parenting

#1 Education is tailor-made
Because the education does not exist and every child is - fortunately! - different.

#2 Connect first, then limit
Because everything starts and ends with unconditional parenthood.

#3 Doing it yourself gives confidence
Because the more you believe in yourself and trust yourself, the more resilient you are in life.

#4 Take care for later
Because parenting is a long-term task, in which children increasingly give direction to their own lives.

#5 The best parents make themselves redundant in the long run
Because strong parents also think about what they need.
