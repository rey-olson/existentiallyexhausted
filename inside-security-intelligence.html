"I didn’t understand the difference between intelligence and knowledge. So I assumed I was an idiot."

Update: not every episode is as interesting as I'd expected. Some episodes resemble a dull sales pitch for the company of the interviewee.

I am not into shilling specific companies or services (apparently I am for certain podcasts), but Recorded Future has quite a nice podcast "Inside Security Intelligence" covering a multitude of cyber threat intelligence topics. 

Each episode is between 20-30 minutes, which is doable to cram in between (or combine with!) household activities.

In an unrelated note, I couldn't find a catchy title, so I just googled "intelligence+quote" and came across the current one: a quote by J.D. Vance from his book "Hillbilly Elegy: A Memoir of a Family and Culture in Crisis".
